"We grow over 150 species of native plants a year, right here — come see how."

— Victor Azzaretto, Horticulture Technician

What we grow

Two heated greenhouses producing native grasses, wildflowers, and plants used across Bethpage's pollinator gardens, golf course landscapes, and restoration sites — plus seed collected and stored for the Community Seed Library.

What a visit looks like

A walk through the greenhouse and nursery, seeing propagation in progress, and a look at where these plants end up — pollinator gardens, the Nature Discovery Garden, and roadside restoration sites.

Why contributing matters

Every logged visit and contribution builds a public record of this garden's growth — the kind of track record that attracts grants, new partners, and program support. Small acts (a visit, a photo, a few hours) add up to real proof.
